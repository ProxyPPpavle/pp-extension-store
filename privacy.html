Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At PP Extension Store, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Email Address: Used solely for account verification and Client ID generation
  • Client ID: A unique identifier for your extensions (stored locally on your device)
  • Usage Credits: Tracked to manage your extension usage limits

We do NOT collect:

  • Browsing history or activity
  • Personal identification documents
  • Payment information (we don't process payments)
  • Location data beyond country-level for ad serving

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Verifying your identity during account setup
  • Generating and managing your unique Client ID
  • Tracking extension usage credits
  • Sending verification codes to your email

We NEVER:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Track your browsing activity

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption:

  • Database: Hosted on Supabase with enterprise-grade security
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Local Storage: Client ID and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • No Viruses: All extensions are scanned and verified to be malware-free

Third-Party Advertising

We use ExoClick for advertising to keep our extensions free. ExoClick may collect:

  • Device information (browser type, OS)
  • Country-level location data
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, views)

ExoClick operates independently and has its own privacy policy. We do not control or have access to the data they collect. You can learn more at ExoClick Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View your Client ID and credits anytime in your profile
  • Delete: Request account deletion by using the "Reset Account" feature
  • Opt-Out: Stop using our services at any time by uninstalling extensions
  • Data Portability: Your Client ID is portable and can be exported

Voluntary Downloads

All downloads from PP Extension Store are 100% voluntary. By downloading our extensions, you acknowledge that:

  • You are downloading software of your own free will
  • You have read and understood the installation instructions
  • Extensions are provided "as-is" for personal use
  • We are not liable for misuse or improper installation

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our website.
